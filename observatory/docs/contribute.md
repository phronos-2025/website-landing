# Contribute to P.H.R.O.N.O.S.

P.H.R.O.N.O.S. is built on the principle of open peer review. We welcome contributions from researchers, practitioners, and anyone interested in strategic foresight.

## How to Contribute

### 1. Submit Feedback

Found an error or have a suggestion? You can:
- [Open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/phronos-observatory/phronos-website/issues)
- Email us at research@phronos.org
- Submit a pull request with proposed changes

### 2. Peer Review

Our documents are living and benefit from expert review:
- Review our theoretical frameworks
- Challenge our empirical findings
- Suggest improvements to methodology
- Identify missing citations

### 3. Contribute Data

Have relevant data or research? We can:
- Integrate your findings
- Link to your work
- Collaborate on joint analysis

## Contributor Recognition

Contributors are acknowledged in:
- Document version histories
- The "Acknowledged Experts" section
- Annual contributor reports

## Guidelines

1. **Be Specific**: Point to exact sections and provide concrete suggestions
2. **Be Constructive**: Frame criticism as opportunities for improvement
3. **Cite Sources**: Back up claims with evidence
4. **Stay Respectful**: Engage in good faith intellectual discourse

Thank you for helping make P.H.R.O.N.O.S. better!
